Research & leadership

where most of this lives

Moore Lab, Undergraduate Researcher

Working on central-pattern-generator dynamics and integrate-and-fire modeling under PI Jeffrey Moore, contributing analysis toward an in-progress publication on rhythmic neural circuits.

  • Computational Modeling: Building bilateral whisking circuit models in mice using integrate-and-fire frameworks in MATLAB, running parameter sweeps over neurophysiological variables.
  • Signal Processing: Applying ISI distributions, autocorrelation, PSTH, and spike-triggered averages to neural recordings.
  • Hardware & Circuitry: Designed and assembled custom camera time-sync circuits and airflow sensor drivers; formalized schematics using TinyCAD.
